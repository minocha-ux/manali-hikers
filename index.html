Our Story · The Family

Trail knowledge passed down, not looked up.

Manoj Kumar's family are Khampa — Buddhist nomads who traced many of the Himalayan passes we still cross today, long before they had names on any map. From those nomadic herders to a certified mountaineer leading guests over the same passes now, it is one continuous line — and every trip still carries that inherited knowledge of the mountains.

Routes worth the altitude.

Every trip is planned around your dates and group — solo, private, or small-group — led by ABVIMAS-trained guides, with acclimatisation days built around real weather, not a fixed itinerary. Priced by conversation, not by catalogue.
