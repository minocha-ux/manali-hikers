Trekking Camp

This is an educational Programme with a tough itinerary and Participants must come prepared to test their Stamina and will power. We presume that each Participant wants to get maximum exposure to the natural elements and there by regain some of the primeval vitality of our ancestors. This Programme, is designed for the gradual acclimatization of the every participants so that on completion of the fortnight there is complete change in personality and attitude.

This training will be very encouraging in building your Child's confidence and will boost his moral. In this Organization the adventurous Spirit of child is nurtured and brought out in a constructive way. Brotherhood, Leadership, problem solving, negotiating, adjustment, friendly environment, sharing with all participants, obedience and disciplinary qualities are inculcated in them which helps in development of the child.
