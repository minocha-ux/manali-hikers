Shetidhar Peak Expedition

The climb  to Shetidhar Peak starting from Manali, this climbing winds through the famous Solang Valley. Its 15 kms from the Manali town. Solang valley is famous for its sky slopes. On this Expedition  you will find lush green meadows, glaciers and snow-capped mountain peaks. The trail then goes via Dhundi and Bakarthach to enter the Beas Kund glacier, the birthplace of the River Beas. From Shetidhar peak you can see very panoramic view of Dholadhar and Pir Panjal mountain range.

Trip Detail

Region: Himachal Pradesh

Max Altitude: 5200 mtr.

Season: 15 April to 30 October

Duration: 11ights/10Days

Age Group: Between 15 to 60 years.

Minimum Numbers per Group: 5

Cost: On Request

ITINERARY:

Day 01): Delhi – Manali

Arrival Manali and rest till afternoon . After lunch visit Hidimba temple. Overnight Hotel

Day 02): Manali

After Breakfast acclimatization trek to old Manali, Goshal village  and Vashist hot spring village. After lunch preparation for the expedition.  Overnight  hotel.

Day 03): Manali – Solang Nallah(2470mts) - Dhundi (3150 mts) Bakarthach (3300 mts)

In the morning after breakfast drive 13km to Solang Nallah. Bakarthach is the base of the all the surrounding peaks like Sh.etidhar (5200m), Ladakhi (5350m), Friendship (5300m) Manali (5600m) hanuman Tibba (5940m). After evening tea acclimatization walk and lecture on surroundings and camp hygiene.overnight tents.

Day 04): Bakarthach (3300 mts)-Beas Kund (3650mts)

Today we will gradually ascending from the valley, the trail continues to the scenic place  at Bakarthach. After 30 minutes  break at Bakarthach trek over moraine to the small lake at Beas Kund, the birth place of the river Beas.

Day 05,06,): Beas Kund

After bfast  move to biaskund glacier. Practice ascending and descending with technique on snow . Back at camp  for lunch. Free till to evening tea.

Day 07): Beaskund-Camp-1(4500mts)

Today we will steep ascending  to Camp- 1  with pack lunch. To reach Camp -1  established the camp. After evening tea  acclimatization walk  and early dinner. Overnight tents.

Day 08,09): Camp-1-Shetidhar Peak(5200mts)-Biaskund

Today we will start  around 02:00am   to climb  Shetidhar Peak. From Camp-1 we start to climb with rope up. We will reach at Peak after 06hours from here you can see very panoramic view of Dholadhar and Pirpanjal range with Indrasan peak(6160mts) and Deotibba Peak (6001mts). After summit the peak we will Back to camp-1.

Day 10): Biaskund-Manali

After breakfast move back to Dhundhi and drive to Manali. Evening free at mall road for own activities.Overnight hotel.

Day 11): Manali-Delhi

Today day spend leisurely at Manali. Evening bus depart for Delhi.
