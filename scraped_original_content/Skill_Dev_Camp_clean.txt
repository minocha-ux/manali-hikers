Adventure

Skill Development Camp

At Camp Manali Outdoor experiential programs are a great way to discover one’s life skills, leadership skills, team spirit and capacity to cope away from the comforts of the known. Providing students with opportunities to learn outdoor and adventure skills is also a common goal, as is the idea of simply giving them the experience of living out of doors.It is an established fact that adventure/outdoor programs in association with nature improves and develops individual performance in various spheres of life and improves one’s abilities to cope with stressful and adverse conditions beyond the camp, in their regular lives.

Trip Detail

Region: Kullu to Manali

Season: Mar to Nov

Duration: 5 to 7 days

Group Size: minimum 20 persons

Leadership: Local Leader

Cost: On Request

ITINERARY:

Day 01:  Arrive in Manali in the morning. Check into Camp Manali.

Day 02:  Trek to Jogini falls/ Khanyal Village and back by evening.

Day 03:  Rock climbing and rappelling. River crossing Post lunch on the Beas or Manalsu.

Day 04:  Today we carry our pack lunch and head in a direction where we will do some snow hiking. We might choose to hire a vehicle to take us to the road head and then do a nice long hike on Snow

Day 05:  Paragliding, Zorbing, short trek at Solang. Return to camp manali by evening.

Day 06:  River Rafting on the Beas.

Day 07:  Depart from Manali after breakfast.

Thing to get:

Backpack / rucksack

Warm track pants / trousers and shirts

Good walking / hiking shoes

Flash light with extra batteries

Sunglasses

Suntan lotion and toiletries

Camera (optional)

Water bottle

Warm jacket / pullover

Warm gloves

Warm socks

Raincoat

Prescribed medication if any

Navigation
