Kinner Kailash Trek

This wonderful trek in Kinnaur completes the circiut around the revered Kinnaur Kailash mountain. Kinnaur was forbidden to trekkers till a few years ago, consequently these trails have been less used. The Kinnaur Kailash Range borders the district of Kinnaur in the south and is dominated by the Kinnaur Kailash (6349m) and Jonkarden (6473m) peaks. The only pass on this trek is the Charang La at an altitude of 5300m

Trip Detail

Region: Himachal Pradesh

Max Altitude: 5300 mtr.

Season: Jun - October

Duration: 07 Days

Group Size: minimum 2 persons, maximum 14 persons.

Leadership: Local Leader

Cost: On Request

ITINERARY:

Day 01: Arrival in Shimla

Day 02: Shimla – Sangla – Thangi

Duration:  5 hours

A long drive to Thangi (2760m) via the district headquarters at Recong Peo.

Day 03: Thangi – Lambar

Duration:  4 hours

Trek through some beautiful alpine scenery to Lambar (2890m). Parts of the trail pass through a grand gorge.

Day 04: Lambar – Charang

Duration:  6 hours

The ascent continues to the substantially large village at Charang (3500m), which is named after the pass nearby.

Day 05: Charang – Lalanti

Duration:  4 hours

The trail continues up till the campsite at Lalanti, at the base of Charang La. The pastures around Lalanti (4420m) are visited by sheperds in the summer months.

Day 06: Lalanti – Charang La – Chitkul

Duration:  8 hours

The trail to the pass is demanding but spectacular views from the top of the pass are assured. The descent from the top is quite steep but the warm camp in Chitkul await.

Day 07: Chitkul – Shimla

Duration:  9 hours

Drive back to Shimla by road
