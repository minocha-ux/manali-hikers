Adventure

Hamta Pass Trek

The trek starts from Prini village of Manali and goes across the Hamta pass up the headwaters of the Chandra river in Lahaul and explores the lower portion of the mighty Bara Shigri glacier. Slopes of the Hamta Pass offers beautiful views of Deo Tibba and Indrasan

Trip Detail

Region: Himachal Pradesh

Located: 32° 14' 09" N. 77° 26' 30" E.

Season: Monsoon Jul - Sep

Duration: 08 Days,Day's on Trek 06 Days,Driving 02 Days

Group Size: minimum 2 persons, maximum 14 persons.

Leadership: Local Leader

Cost: On Request

ITINERARY:

DAY 01

Delhi -Manali

Delhi – Anandpur Sahiab overnight Himachal Express Train Drive Anandpur Sahiab – Manali (1960m) Overnight hotel in Manali

DAY 02

Trek Manali -Chika

Manali to Jobri naala by jeep After that 2hrs gradual walk. Uphill and downhill crossing few streams to Hampta River.Overnight Camp at Chika

DAY 03

Trek Chikha -Balu Ghera

Chikha -Balu Ghera (3700m) 3-4 hrs shot up and downhill. Overnight camp at Balu Ghera

DAY 04

Trek Balu Ghera - Hamta Pass-Shiyagaru

We cross Hampta Pass and descend to basecamp on the other side into Chandra valley. You will get great views of Deo Tibba and Indrasan peaks as well as the Lahaul valleys. Overnight Camp Shiyagaru.

DAY 05

Trek Shiyagaru –Chatru - manali

Shiyagaru -Chatru (3360m) 3-4 hrs Couple of river crossing with descent for 3-4hrs.

Drive Chatru - Manali (1960m) 2-3 hrs
