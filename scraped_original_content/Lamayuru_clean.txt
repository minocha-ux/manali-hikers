Lamayuru Trek

Trip Detail

Region: Ladakh

Max Altitude:  5120mtr

Season: May – October

Duration: 25 Days

HIGH PASSES:  Singe la (5120m.

Leadership: Local Leader

Cost: On Request

ITINERARY:
DAY 01: Arrival Delhi.

DAY 02: Delhi to Manali (2000 m.) 570 kms - 16 hours - by night tourist bus.

DAY 03: Manali

DAY 04: Manali to Darcha-Palamo (3600 m) 150 km

DAY 05: Palamo to Zanskar Sumdo (3950 m) - 6 hours - Trek 1

DAY 06: Zanskar Sumdo to Chhumik Marpo (4500 m) - 7.30 hours - Trek 2

DAY 07: Chhumik Marpo to Lahkhang (4300 m) over Shingo la (5090 m) - 7 to 8 hours - Trek 3

DAY 08: Lhakhang to Kargyak (4060 m) - 5 hours - Trek 4

DAY 09: Kargyak to Purni (3800 m) - 6 hours - Trek 5

DAY 10: Purni to Phucktal back to Purni - 4 hours to and fro. - Trek. 6

DAY 11: Purni to Pipula (3600 m) - 5 hours - Trek 7

DAY 12: Pipula to Raru via Ichar (3700 m) - 5.30 hours - Trek 8

DAY 13: Raru to Padum (3600 m) - 6 hours - Trek 9

DAY 14: Padum to Rinam (3500 m) - 6 hours - Trek 10

DAY 15: Rinam to Pishu (3400 m) 3 hours, via Jangla - 6 hours - Trek 11

DAY 16: Pishu to Hanumil (3550 m) - 5 hours - Trek 12

DAY 17: Hanumil to Snertse (4200 m) over Parfila (3900 m) - 6 hours - Trek 13

DAY 18: Snertse to Lingshed (3800 m)over Hanumala (4700 m) - 7 hours - Trek 14

DAY 19: Lingshed to Yulchun gyra (4500 m) Trek starts over Murgum la (4100 m) - Trek 15

DAY 20: Yulchungyra to Phutoksar (3750 m) over singe la (5120m) - 7 hours - Trek 16

DAY 21: Phutoksar to Hanupata (3500 m) Over Sirsila (4815 m) - 6 hours - Trek 17

DAY 22: Hanupata to Wanla (3300 m) - 5 hours - Trek 18

DAY 23: Wanla to Lamayuru/Leh via Alchi (3600 m) over Pritink la (3800 m) - End of the Trek 19

DAY 24: Visit Shey ThikseyHemis.

DAY 25: Leh-Delhi flight.
