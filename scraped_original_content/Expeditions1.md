Expeditions
        The Himalayas contain some of the greatest mountain peaks in the world. Himachal Pradesh, Uttaranchal and Ladakh are home to numerous peaks most of them above 6000 meters. Manali Hikers based in the Manali in Himachal Pradesh offer you total ground logistic support for mountaineering expeditions in Himachal Pradesh and Ladakh to make your expedition a success. The best season for climbing in the Indian Western Himalayan is from May to early October. Zanskar and Ladakh do not experience the India monsoon as they fall under the rain shadow region of Himalayas hence making for ideal expeditions in the region during June, July, August and September.
