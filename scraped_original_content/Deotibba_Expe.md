Deotibba Expedition
        Deo Tibba is a beautiful 6001metre high peak  situated in the Pir Panjal range of mountains in the Manali area in Himachal  Pradesh. Deo Tibba consists of an extensive ice cap the actual climb being a  snow hump accessible once the edge of the ice plateau is reached. It also has  the beauty of being just over the 6000 meter high mark which is a great  achievement for any climber. 

          Trip Detail

            **Region:** Himachal Pradesh

            **Max Altitude:**6001 mtr.

            **Season:**15 April  to  30 October 

            **Duration:**13Nights/14Days 

            **Age Group:**Between 15 to 60 years.

            **Minimum Numbers per Group:**10

            **Cost:**On Request

  **ITINERARY:**

**Day 01):** **Delhi**** – Manali**

    Arrival Manali and rest in the Hotel . Overnight  Hotel

  **Day 02):** **Manali **

    After Breakfast visit the Hadimba temple, the Vashisht hot spring water & the other interesting places of Manali. Night in hotel. Overnight  hotel.

  **Day 03):** **Manali  – Ad hydro project tunnel point - Chikka (3350 mts)**

    In the morning after breakfast drive 12km   to AD tunnel point  and from here  trek 03 km to Chikka. Set out camp for overnight tents.

  **Day 04):** **Chikka  - Seri (3800 mts)  **

    Today we will gradually 08 km ascending  from the valley, the trail continues to the scenic  campsite at Seri. overnight tents.

  **Day 05):** **Seri –  Chandertaal-Deotibba Base(4480 mts)**

    After breakfast trek over moraine to the small lake at Chandertaal(Base of  Deotibba Peak-6001mts), the birth place of the river Dhugan. 

  **Day 06 & Day 07): ****Deotibba Base**

    After bfast move to Deotibba glacier for practice ascending and descending with  all the techniques on snow and ice. . After evening tea preparation and discussion for next day.  Overnight tents. 

  **Day 08):** **Deotibba  Base-Camp-1(5200mts)**

    Today we will ascend to camp-1 with gradual walk and last 120 m with fix rope  climbing. Camp set at camp -1, early dinner and Overnight tents.

  **Day 09):**** Camp-1-Summit(6001mts)  –Camp-1**

    Early morning around 02:00am start to climb peak. After some gradual climbing,  150m gully climbing with fix rope. And then again some gradual climbing and  last   50 m fix rope for summit. Climb  peak and descend safe back to camp-1.Overnight tents.** **

**Day 10): Camp-1-Base camp**

    Today we descend to base camp. Free rest of the day. Overnight tents.    

**Day 11& 12): Base Camp-Chikka**

    Extra day for bad weather. 

**Day 13): Chikka-Tunnel point-Manali**

    Easy day and trek to tunnel point. From here drive to Manali. Overnight hotel.

** Day 14): Manali-Delhi**

    Today we visit Manali town for some shopping and evening depart for Delhi.
