Mountain Biking in Leh-Ladakh

The 490 km (306 mile) expedition from Manali to Leh (Ladakh) (or in reverse) is one of the ultimate adventures for any experienced mountain biker.
Ladakh, situated above the Indus River at 12,000ft and isolated deep within the Karakoram and Zangskar mountain ranges, provides the most spectacular view and myriad mountain scenery.
Crossing the Himalayas from south to north, the route traverses four of the world’s highest passes, including the TaglangLa at 5328-m (world's 2nd highest motor able pass).

Trip Detail

Season: July through mid – August.

Duration: 17 days/16 nights tour

Leadership: Local Leader

Minimum Group Size: 6 people

Cost: On Request

ITINERARY:

Day 1 Fly to Delhi. Groups meet and train to Chandigargh bus to Manali, Separate vehicles for bikes & luggage direct to Manali.

Day 2 Arrive Manali- unpack gear and settle into hotel and rest.

Day 3 Manali. Rest. Unpack bikes and assemble/check

Day 4 Ride / Camp at Marni, Rothang La at 3978m alt.

Day 5 Ride / Camp near Koksa

Day 6 Ride / Camp near Jispa (3142m alt) Via Keylong 3185m alt.

Day 7 Ride / Camp before Baralach La near Patseo.

Day 8 Ride / Camp near Sarchu Via Baralach La 4883m alt.

Day 9 Ride / Camp near Pang Via Lachlung La Pass- 5065m alt.

Day 10 Ride/Pang 4885m alt.

Day 11 Ride to More Plateau (More Plains) Camp.

Day 12 Ride / Camp near Taglang La Pass 5328m alt.

Day 13 Ride / Camp near Upshi.

Day 14 Ride to Stok Village Stok Highland Hotel (Leh 3523m alt.)

Day 15 Tours or Ride – Indus Valley or Flex Day.

Day 16 Ride to Khardong La Pass & return to Stok for packing.

Day 17 Fly Leh-Delhi on IC#481. Intern. Airport transfer to Hotel in Delhi. Arrival in Delhi airport from Leh approx 10:30-11:00am
