      Rafting        

      Rafting in River Beas(White Water River Rafting)

        White water rafting is another   				adventure attraction at Manali, Tourist coming to visit Manali   				on Holiday  or Honeymoon, Everyone is interested in White   				water rafting in Manali. White water rafting is done in river Beas at Manali , Beas   				river is good for rafting , kayaking, canoeing etc. Rafting is   				done in Beas river from  Manali to Jhiri section near   				Bajaura in Kullu district , Rafting has become quite popular   				among the water sports lovers, tourists and corporate groups.   				Rafting season  starts from mid April to mid-June and rest   				depends on the arrival of monsoon.
