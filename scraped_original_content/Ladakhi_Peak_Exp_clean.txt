Ladakhi Peak Expedition

The climb  to Ladakhi Peak starting from Manali, this climbing winds through the famous Solang Valley. Its 15 kms from the Manali town. Solang valley is famous for its sky slopes. On this Expedition  you will find lush green meadows, glaciers and snow-capped mountain peaks. The trail then goes via Dhundi and Bakarthach to enter the Beas Kund glacier, the birthplace of the River Beas. From Ladakhi peak you can see very panoramic view of Dholadhar, Pir Panjal mountain range and many peaks which are above 6000mts.Ladakhi is approached from the same ridge as Shetidhar and the last 50 meters of the climb involves a 5.6 grade rock climb to the summit.

Trip Detail

Region: Himachal

Max Altitude: 6000 mtr.

Season: 15 April  to 30 October

Duration: 10Nights/11Days

Age Group: Between 15 to 60 years.

Minimum Numbers per Group: 10

Cost: On Request

ITINERARY:

Day 01): Delhi – Manali

Arrival Manali and rest till afternoon . After lunch visit Hidimba temple. Overnight Hotel

Day 02): Manali

After Breakfast acclimatization trek to old Manali, Goshal village and Vashist hot spring village. After lunch preparation for the expedition. Overnight hotel.

Day 03): Manali – Solang Nallah(2470mts) - Dhundi (3150 mts) Bakarthach (3300 mts)

In the morning after breakfast drive 13km to Solang Nallah. Bakarthach is the base of the all the surrounding peaks like Sh.etidhar (5200m), Ladakhi (5350m), Friendship (5300m) Manali (5600m) hanuman Tibba (5940m). After evening tea acclimatization walk and lecture on surroundings and camp hygiene.overnight tents.

Day 04): Bakarthach (3300 mts)-Beas Kund (3650mts)

Today we will gradually ascending from the valley, the trail continues to the scenic place at Bakarthach. After 30 minutes break at Bakarthach trek over moraine to the small lake at Beas Kund, the birth place of the river Beas.

Day 05,06,): Beas Kund

After bfast move to biaskund glacier. Practice ascending and descending with technique on snow . Back at camp for lunch. Free till to evening tea.

Day 07): Beaskund-Camp-1(4500mts)

Today we will steep ascending to Camp- 1 with pack lunch. To reach Camp -1 established the camp. After evening tea acclimatization walk and early dinner. Overnight tents.

Day 08): Camp-1-Ladakhi Peak (5536)-Camp-1

Shetidhar peak is only 30minutes climbing from here and Ladakhi is around 2hrs climbing from col. And after col we fix the rope and will climb fix rope, where we have to do some rock climbing from here you can see very panoramic view of Dholadhar and Pirpanjal range with Indrasan peak(6160mts) and Deotibba Peak (6001mts). After summit the peak we will Back to camp-1. Overnight tents.

Day 09): Camp-1-Bakarthach

After breakfast move back to Beaskund. And after lunch free in camp. Evening celebration with bonfire. Overnight tents.

Day 10): Bakarthach-Solang-Manali

After breakfast trek to Solang Valley and from here drive to Manali. Evening free for shopping at Manali. Overnight Hotel.

Day 11): Manali-Departure

Early morning transfer to Bus stand at  Manali.
