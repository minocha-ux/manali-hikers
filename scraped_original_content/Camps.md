Camping
      
        Camping is perhaps one  of the easiest ways of enjoying the wonders that nature has given Himachal and  sharing in the warm hospitality of its people.    We offer organized camps that offer accommodation, catering and a  variety of activities like hikes, nature tours, rock climbing, rappelling, river  rafting, river crossing, snow skiing, etc. All these activities are managed &   organized under able  guidance of a Mountaineering Instructor who is   responsible for day to day  running of the camp. Lessons on basics of   camping, trekking and other adventure  sports are imparted by the   instructor.Adventure  camp is not only all about camping &   adventure activities but it also  provides you the opportunity to   appreciate the nature, village, people, culture  & tradition,   beautiful landscape,   flora & fauna and living in harmony with   nature. Well trained  Instructor will teach the students the technical   points of activities like Rock  climbing, Rappling, River Crossing,   Trekking, Camping. All these activities  will help the students in   developing certain skills like leadership qualities,  management skills,   co-operation, crises management, mental toughness, problem  solving,   physical toughness and positive attitude in life.
