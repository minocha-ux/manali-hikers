**About us**

          Hi my name is Manoj Kumar.
I am the owner of Manali Hikers.
I have been grown up in the remote area of North East Himalayan region. It is pleasure to introduce ourselves as an adventure company. My story of being a Hiker starts with my ancestors. Our family belongs to Khampa community & they belong to Buddhist Religion. In the old times they are basically Nomads.They had great journeys in the Himalayan regions with their cattle and with their family. So they had found a great number of trek routes in Himalaya. Not going on the footprints of their ancestors my father settled himself at Shaminala near Jagatsukh Himachal Pradesh.

  After that for living my father started doing trekking on those trek   routes. I also participated on those expeditions. I also had done those   routes with dozens of times my own. Going with the legacy given to me by   my father, I started my own company the Manali Hikers. That is   registered with Himachal Tourism. My qualifications in  mountaineering,   Advance & MOI (Method of Instruction)     from (ABVIMAS), Manali. I’ve climbed more than 10 peaks between   6,000-6,500. Our company has a big number of experienced guides. They   work together as a team and they take their difficulties as challenges.          Every individual in this adventure loving team are technically   perfect in all adventure related activities.Our equipments are best   available in the market.    Manali Hiker   guarantees you the most competitive rates on all our tourism related   products.
