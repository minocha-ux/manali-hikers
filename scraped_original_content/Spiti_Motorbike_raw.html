Spiti Valley MotorBike Tour
This tour takes you up to the incredible Spiti Valley riding through the Banjar Valley, Pin Valley& into Kinnaur. Some of the highlights include the tunning Bhimakali Temple in Sarahan and the beautiful village of Nako on the Tibetian /Chinese border.
Trip Detail
Season:Summer / June - october Destination: Manali- Naggar- Solang Valley- Banjar Valley (Soja)- Sarahan (over Jalori Pass)- Kalpa- Naco- Kaza(over Kunzam Pass)-Chatru (camping) – Keylon (over Rohtang Pass) –Manali
Duration: 10 days/9 nights tour
Leadership: Local Leader
Minimum Group Size: 6 people

ITINERARY:
Day 1 Manali (2000mtrs): On arrival you will be warmly welcomed at Bhuntar airport (if you are flying up from Delhi) and checked into a very comfortable hotel just outside Manali. You will meet the team and have an opportunity to do some local sightseeing depending on the time you arrive.
Day 2 Manali Today you will be introduced to your Royal Enfield motorbike and have a chance to explore the local area. We ride up to the Solang Valley and out to Naggar Castle. There is a chance to visit the Nicholas Roerich museum and then visit some Hindu temples and see the Vashisht Hot Springs.
Day 3 Manali to Banjar Valley The first day takes us South towards Bajar Valley to Soja (2683 mts).From Manali we take a ride till Bhuntar where we take left turn to Parvati Valley, following Parvati river we reach Manikaran (temple complex with hot springs), have a lunch a go back to Bhuntar where aim to beautifull Bajar Valley, enjoying views around finely we reach Soja village (6km from Bajar) stay night here.
Day 4 Banjar to Sarahan (2165 mtrs)- over Jarori Pass(3134mtrs) We aim to arrive into Sarahan mid afternoon enabling a tour of the stunning Bhimakali temple. This Temple a sacred temple of the Hindus and is an architectural marvel. It is believed to be at least 800 years old.
Day 5 Sarahan to Kalpa (3300 mtrs) There are a number of temples to see in Kalpa and we stay in a comfortable hotel and enjoy the views.
Day 6 Kalpa to Nako (3663mtrs) Naco is a small village in Kinnaur and at an altitude of 3663 meters.Lake Nako in the centre of the village, formed out of the masses of ice and snow is a highlight of the Naco is dotted with at least seven temples of different periods , including a monastic complex on its western edge.
Day 7 Naco to Kaza (3700mtrs) On the way to Kaza we will pass Dankar monastery(an ancient monastery build 12 century, over a ridge. Stay night in hotel in Kaza and next morning take a ride to Key Monastery-the oldest and biggest monastic complex standing on the hill in the Spiti valley)/Kibar (4400),8km from Key village-highest village in the world with a motor able road and electricity)/evening come back to Kaza/email phone calls.
Day 8 Kaza to Chutru (camping) Today we take a day ride from the Kaza to the Chandatar Lake, have a lunch, enjoy the view, and after rest get on riding to Chutru(camping).
Day 9 Chutru to Keylong (3080 mtrs) nice hotel overlooking the valley.
Day 10 Keylong to Manali
Today last Day of tour. At Evening Farewell Dinner. Sharing emotions after journey. Hot Spring in Vashisht for relaxation. Shopping in manali, some gifts for friends. Presents from our Company for memory. Next Morning Transfer to Bhuntar Aiport, Flight to Delhi.
