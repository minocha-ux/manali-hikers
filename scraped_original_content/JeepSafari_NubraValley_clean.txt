Nubra Valley Jeep Safari Tour

Journey to Nubra Valley north of Leh is special for several reasons. First it is accessed by the highest motor able road in the world traversing the stunning Khardung La at the thin air of 5600m above sea level with views of the Saser Spur, the easternmost outcrop of the Karakoram further north and the Zanskar range and the principal chain of the Himalayas to the south. Second, from the awesome highs we descend to a picturesque valley with an average altitude of 3000 meters and thus a milder climate than the rest of Ladakh. And third, this valley once constituted the last staging ground for the caravan trade with Central Asia.

ITINERARY:

Day 01: Arrive Delhi

Upon arrival at Delhi transfer to hotel for overnight stay.

Day 02: Delhi - Leh

Early morning transfer to domestic airport to catch flight for Leh. Upon arrival at Leh transfer to hotel. The day is for acclimatization. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 03: Leh - Shey - Thiksey - Hemis

On this day you will go a sightseeing trip to some ancient monasteries in this area.

Shey Gompa is about 15 kms from Leh. It used to be the summer palace of the kings of Ladakh. There are lots of Stupas and Gompas built around the palace. Shey palace was built by Deldan Namgyal in the beginning of 17'Th century AD. Main attraction in Shey is the 12m Shakyamuni Buddha statue inside the Dresthang Gompa.

Thiksey Gompa , close to Shey is about 17 kms from Leh. The monastery is considered to be one of the most beautiful in Ladakh and belongs to the Gelukspa order.

Hemis , about 45 kms south of Leh it is one of the most famous and largest monastery in Ladakh. It belongs to the Drukpa order and was founded in the early 17'Th century. The setting is perfect with the monastery cradled in a lovely valley, surrounded by streams and fronted by long Mani walls. Overnight stay at the guest house.

Day 04: Leh - Panamik

In the morning after breakfast we will drive to Panamik via Gangles village, then crossing over Khardong La. Enter Nubra valley and then along the Nubra River camp at Panamik.

Day 05: Panamik - Ansa - Panamik

Morning visit Ansa monastery on a hilltop across the Nubra River. Afternoon visit Panamik village & the hot springs. Overnight stay.

Day 06: Panamik - Kyagar - Samstanling - Sumur - Diskit

Drive to Diskit enroute visit Samstanling monastery & sumur monastery Overnight camp at Diskit.

Day 07: Diskit - Hunder - Diskit

Morning drive to Hunder & visit the Monastery. Later explore the town of Diskit, headquarter of Nubra.

Day 08: Diskit - Leh

Leave Diskit after breakfast. Arrive Leh in the evening. Overnight at hotel.

Day 09: Leh

In the morning after breakfast visit Phyang, Spituk and Stok Museum. Overnight stay.

Day 10: Leh - Delhi

In the morning after breakfast take an early morning flight to Delhi. Arrive and transfer to hotel. Overnight stay.

Day 11: Depart Delhi

Drive to airport to catch onward flight according your flight schedule.
