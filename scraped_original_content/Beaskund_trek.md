Beaskund Trek
        The trek to Beas Kund starting from Manali, this trek winds through the  famous Solang Valley. Its 15 kms from the Manali town.  Solang valley is famous for its sky slopes. On this trek you will find lush  green meadows,glaciers and snow-capped mountain peaks. The trail then goes via  Dhundi and Bakarthach to enter the Beas Kund glacier, the birthplace of the  River Beas.

          Trip Detail

            **Region:** Himachal Pradesh

            **Season:**Monsoon Jul - Sep

            **Duration:** 06 Days

            **Group Size:**minimum 2 persons, maximum 14 persons.

            **Leadership:**Local Leader

            **Cost:**On Request

  **ITINERARY:

  Day 01):** ** Manali**

Arrival Manali and rest till afternoon . After lunch visit Hidimba  temple, old Manali,  Vashist hot spring village.Overnight  hotel.

**Day 02):** **Manali – Solang Nallah(2470mts) - Dhundi (3150 mts) Bakarthach (3300 mts)**

In the morning after breakfast drive 13km to Solang Nallah and dhundi is 6km from Solang.  Bakarthach  is the base of the all the surrounding peaks like Sh.etidhar (5200m), Ladakhi (5350m), Friendship (5300m) Manali (5600m) hanuman Tibba (5940m). After evening tea acclimatization walk and lecture on surroundings and camp hygiene.overnight tents.

**Day 03):** **Bakarthach (3300 mts)-Beas Kund (3650mts) **

Today we will gradually ascending from the valley, the trail  continues to the scenic place  at  Bakarthach. After 30 minutes  break at  Bakarthach trek over moraine to the small lake at Beas Kund, the birth place of  the river Beas.     

 **Day  04):** **Beas**** Kund **

today we will take easy walk and come back. 

**Day 05):****Beas**** Kund -Manali **

After breakfast trek to Dhundi and from here  drive to Manali. Evening free for shopping at Manali. Overnight Hotel.

**Day 06):**** Manali-Departure**

  Evening transfer to Bus stand at  Manali.
