Trekking
      Manali Hikers  organizes treks in the different parts of   western Himalaya,  ( whole of Himachal Pradesh including Kullu valley,   Parvati valley, Lahoul,  Spiti, Kinnaur, Sangla, Dharam Shala ,& Ladakh). A himalayan trek is a great way to de-stress and bond with your family   and friends or maybe just be on your own; away from it all. Whatever   your needs we are sure the Himalayas will provide. However going on a   Himalayan trek can be an overwhelming experience logistically and even   more so if your chosen destination is the Indian Himalayas with all its   wilderness and low trekking support infrastructure. Here’s where all our   experience comes in handy. We can help with all the logistics and   support you will need right from choosing your Himalayan trek to running   the trek in the field as planned.
