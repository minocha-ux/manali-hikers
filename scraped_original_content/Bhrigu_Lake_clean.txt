Bhrigu Trek

Bhrigu (4240mts.) is a prominent lake of kullu region, located right of Rohtang pass. Legend holds that Bhrigu Rishi meditated here and many local gods of the kullu region take a dip in its sacred water, most prominent among them is Guru vashisht, who travel to the lake at least once in two or three years cycle. A natured forest of Fir, oak and higher altitude white Oak surrounds Gulaba from where trek starts.Steep ups and downs can make you a bit tired but are equally rewarding because of its breathtaking views.

Trip Detail

Region: Himachal Pradesh

Max Altitude: 4235 mtr.

Season: Monsoon Jul - Sep

Duration: 02 Days,Day's on Trek 01 Day,Driving 02 hrs

Group Size: minimum 2 persons, maximum 14 persons.

Leadership: Local Leader

Cost: On Request

ITINERARY:

Day 01): Manali – Gulaba-Kothi Theli (2900 mts)

In the morning after breakfast drive  15km. up to the Manali Rohtang highway to the meadows of Gulaba. The views of the upper kullu valley from here are quite spectacular. The trail toward the lake goes east ward and climb through the dence forest of Deodar the cross a small stream to the camp site of kothi Theli.Set out camp for overnight tents.

Day 02): Kothi Theli – Brighu Lake (4240 mts) –Kothi Theli

Today We ascent towards the lake slowly as the altitude can make you a little breathless. we cross a mountain stream and then keep climbing till we cross a mountain ridge beyond which lies the Bhrigu lake. We reach the lake and after spend some time trek back  to kothi Theli and set the camp. Overnight tents

Day 03): Kothi Theli (2900 mts)

After the breathtaking views of the Bhrigu lake area  We walk back to Gulaba and drive back to Manali.

Customer Itinerary

DAY 02 :Kothi Theli – Brighu Lake
