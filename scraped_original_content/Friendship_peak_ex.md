Friendship peak Expedtion
        One of our most favorite areas for mountaineering, Ski mountaineering and Ski touring is the Beaskund glacier. The area is approached form Manali. Friendship peak in the Beas Kund area is an ideal non-technical or trekking peak for those with limited or no mountaineering knowledge. The nearest road head is Solang nulla in Manali.

          Trip Detail

            **Region:** Himachal Pradesh

            **Max Altitude:**5289m

            **Season:**March to November

            **Duration:**07 Days,06 Nights

            **Group Size:**minimum 2 persons, maximum 14 persons.

            **Leadership:**Local Leader

            **Cost:**On Request

  **ITINERARY:**

**Day 01:** Arrival in Manali

**Day 02:** In the morning after breakfast drive 13km to Solang Nallah. Solang is hub of the adventure activities like paragliding, skiing, camping etc.and from here 6km by jeep to Dhundi.

**Day 03:** After bfast  move to biaskund glacier. Practice ascending and descending with technique on snow & Ice . Back at camp  for lunch. Free till to evening tea. After tea preparation for next day (distribution the dry fruits )  .  Overnight tents.

**Day 04:** Today we will steep ascending  to Camp- 1  with pack lunch. To reach Camp -1  established the camp. After evening tea  acclimatization walk  and early dinner. Overnight tents.

**Day 05:** Rest day and acclimatization day.

**Day 06:** Today we will start  around 02:00am   to climb  friendship  Peak. From Camp-1 we start to climb with rope up. We will reach at Peak after 06hours from here you can see very panoramic view of Dholadhar and Pirpanjal range with Indrasan peak(6160mts) and Deotibba Peak (6001mts). After summit the peak we will Back to camp-1. Overnight tents.

**Day 07:** After breakfast move back to Dhundhi and drive to Manali. Evening free at mall road for own activities.Overnight hotel.

**Customer Itinerary**

        DAY 01 :  Arrival in Manali

        DAY 02 :  Manali – Solang Nallah(2470mts) - Dhundi (3150 mts) Bakarthach (3300 mts)

        DAY 03 :  Bakarthach-Beas Kund (3600mts)-Bakarthach

        DAY 04 :  Bakarthach-Camp-1(4500mts) 

        DAY 05 :  Rest day and acclimatization day.

        DAY 06 :   Camp-1-Friendship Peak(5300mts)-Camp-1

        Day 07 :Camp-1- Bakarthach-Manali
