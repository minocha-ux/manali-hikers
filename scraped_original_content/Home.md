Welcome to Manali Hikers
      Welcome to the exciting world of *Manali Hikers*. Our   area of operation comprises the mountain states of the Himalayas   mainly Himachal Pradesh, Jammu & Kashmir and Uttarakhand. From Manali you can easily access the remote   regions of Spiti, Zanskar, Ladakh and Kashmir as well as cross over   into the beautiful mountains of neighbouring Uttarakhand.    The Manali Hikers today offer you some of the most   exciting journeys and exploration opportunities in this captivating land of Himalaya.Here you will enjoy off-the-beaten-path trekking, mountaineering,   cycling, ski touring and camping holidays across the Indian Himalayas,   in the process re-discovering yourself. Traveling with us means an   unforgettable journey to impossible heights, hidden valleys, remote   glaciers, exploring indigenous cultures and the adventures they offer.   This is our backyard where we have offered active experiential journeys   to discerning travelers since 1998 and yes, we wear our passion on our   sleeves.
