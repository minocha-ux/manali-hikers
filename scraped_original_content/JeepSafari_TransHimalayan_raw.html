Trans Himalayan Jeep Safari Tour
This Jeep safari along the ancient Trans - Himalayan trade to obscure village and Monasteries is an exciting new adventure holiday that packs so much into two weeks. Start your journey from Delhi via Shimla, to a beautiful Sangla valley in the region known as Kinnaur. There is a startling change in the landscape as you enter the district of Spiti Valley. Spending one each night at Tabo, Kaza (head quarter of Spiti valley) & Losar include side trips to Dankar Gompa and the highest village of Kibber (4200 m). The people of Spiti valley have close affinities with Zanskar and Ladakh who are dedicated to the pre-Buddhist Bon religion. Driving out of the Spiti valley enter into Ladakh region

ITINERARY:
Day 01: Delhi - Shimla (375 kms/7 - 8 hrs)
In the morning after early breakfast drive to Shimla by Toyota Qualis. Lunch enroute. Evening free to explore the Mall Road.
Day 02: Shimla
Morning visit Indian Institute of Advanced Studies built in 1888. Also visit the Jakho temple.
Day 03: Shimla - Sarhan (184 kms/ 7 hrs)
We start our jeep safari to Sarhan Enroute; we visit the palace in Rampur. Sarhan, the tiny village offers the splendid views of Shrikhanda Mahadev Peak (5155m).
Sarhan (2125 m): The major attraction in Sarahan is centuries-old Bhimkali Temple and Raja Bushair Palace.
Day 04: Sarhan - Sangla (304 kms/ 8 hrs)
Drive along the spectacular Hindustan Tibet road which s carved in a rock and goes along the wild Sutlej River.
Sangla Valley : This valley starts 57 kms. Short of Kalpa which has been named after a beautiful & populous village Sangla. . A temple dedicated to Nages god is worth a visit & other places are Sapni, Rackchham, Kilba, Kamru Fort which can also be visited.
Day 05: Sangla - Kalpa (53 kms/ 2 hrs)
Morning visit Kamru village and fort. Later travel by surface from Sangla to Kalpa located in backdrop of Kinner Kailash mountain range.
Kalpa (2759 m): Beyond Recong Peo (14 kms. from Powari) on the link road, is the main village of the District - Kalpa.
Day 06: Kalpa - Tabo (176 kms/ 8 hrs)
Travel by surface from Kalpa to Tabo enroute visiting Nako village beautifully located on banks of a small lake.
Tabo Monastery : It is another big gompa for serving the population of eastern side. It belongs to the tenth century and is located 50 kms from Kaza.
Day 07: Tabo - Kaza (50 kms/ 5 hrs)
Morning visit Tabo monastery. Later travel to Kaza enroute visiting Lalung and Dhankar Gompa.
Dhankar Monastery : It is situated about 25 kms. East of Kaza and serves eastern part of central Spiti. Dhankar is a big village and erstwhile capital of Spiti Kingdom.
Day 08 : Kaza
Morning visit the Ki monastery, 12 kms from Kaza.
Ki Monastery : It is situated 12 kms north of Kaza and serves the western population of Spiti. It is the oldest and biggest monastery of the valley and located at (4116 m) above Ki village.
Kibber (4205 m): It is locally known as Khyipur, one of the highest villages in the world at an altitude of 4205 m above sea level in a narrow valley surrounded by mountains from all sides.
Day 09: Kaza - Keylong (206 kms /9 hrs)
In the morning drive to Keylong via Kunzam Pass (4558 m/14,950ft) which provides beautiful views of CB ranges.
Keylong (3340 m): Keylong is the district Headquarters of Lahaul Spiti on the main road to Leh over Rohtang..
Day 10: Keylong - Sarchu (248 kms/8 hrs)
Today we will drive from Keylong to Sarchu over Baralachala Pass (4800 m). On arrival check in at camp at Sarchu.
Day 12: Sarchu - Leh (248 kms/ 8 hrs)
Early morning drive to Leh gives you a feeling of being in the high Tibetan Plateau.
Leh : Leh is the fascinating capital of the Autonomous Hill Council of Ladakh, also known as Little Tibet. The important monasteries to visit in and around Leh are Shey, Thiksey, Hemis, Spituk, Samkhar as well as the Shanti Stupa, Stok Palace and main Bazaar. It takes about 3 days to explore Leh.
Day 15: Leh - Khardung La - Leh (40 kms/2 hrs)
In the morning drive for the Khardung - La Pass. It is the highest motorable road in the world taking you to a dizzy 5,600m!
Day 16: Leh - Delhi
Early in the morning transfer to airport. From here catch a flight for Delhi.
