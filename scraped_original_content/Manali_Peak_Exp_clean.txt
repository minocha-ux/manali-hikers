Manali Peak

Manali Peak is located in the Pir Panjal range in Himachal Pradesh in the Beas Valley. The closest village is Solang Nala located in the Solang Valley, 13 km. north of Manali. The Height of the Peak is about 5640 meter.

Trip Detail

Region: Himachal Pradesh

Located: 32° 14' 09" N. 77° 26' 30" E.

Max Altitude: 5640m/18900 ft

Season: Monsoon Jul - Sep

Duration: 08 Days, 07 Nights

Group Size: minimum 2 persons, maximum 14 persons.

Leadership: Local Leader

Cost: On Request

ITINERARY:

DAY 01

Arrival in Manali (1990m)

Arrival in Manali transfer to hotel. After breakfast get the equipment for the climb sorted out. Fit yourself in your climbing gear (climbing boots, crampons and your harness).
After lunch visit the Hadimba temple, the Vashisht hot spring water. Night in hotel
DAY 02

Manali -Bakkar Thach trek 1

Drive to Dhundi and walk through the forest of Pine, Mapple, Walnut, Silver fir and Oak. Its a Gradual uphill 2-3hrs walk. West of Dhundi the highest mountain of the Dhauladhar ( Hanuman Tibba 5950 m. ) is erected, on the east (Deo Tibba 6001m, and Indra Asan 6100 m. ) can be seen if the weather is in your favor. Bakkar Thach is high altitude sheperd fields-please do see.

DAY 03

Bakkar Thach - Beas Kund (Base Camp)

This is short climb up the ridge and then plateau of BEAS KUND.Shift of camp to Beas Kund depends on good weather.
Packed lunch will be provided for a full day excursion in Beas Kund plateu.

DAY 04

Acclimatization Day

DAY 05

Beas Kund- Camp1

Beas Kund to Camp1 is a 6-7hrs steep uphill walk which includes scenic view all around the trek.

DAY 06

Camp1-Col (Camp2)

Climb to camp2 will take about 5-6hrs. Climb includes technical skills with all climbing gears.Shetidhar peak is only 30minutes climbing from here and Manali Peak is around 3-4hrs climbing from col.

DAY 07

Summit Attempt – Manali Peak 5640m/18900 ft

And after col we fix the rope and will climb fix rope, where we have to do some rock climbing from here you can see very panoramic view of Dholadhar
and Pirpanjal range with Indrasan peak(6160mts) and Deotibba Peak (6001mts). After summit the peak we will Back to camp-1.
Overnight tents.
DAY 08

Camp1-Bakkar Thach

After breakfast trek to Solang Valley and from here drive to Manali. Evening free for shopping at Manali. Overnight Hotel.

DAY 09

Bakkar Thach -Manali

Day 10

Extra Day for Weather

Day 11

Departure from Manali
