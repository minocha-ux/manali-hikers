Lahaul–Spiti Jeep Safari

⏱ 13 days ▲ 4,558m Easy
Overview

Lahaul is a land of high, uniform mountains and massive glaciers, its Chandra and Bhaga valleys rich in Buddhist art and monastic culture, with ancient murals, thangkas and images of Padmasambhava. Summer brings cool, pleasant weather with green grass and alpine flowers, making July to September the ideal window for this overland route via Keylong and Kaza.

Day by day

Day 1 216m

Arrive Delhi

Transfer to hotel, overnight stay.

Day 2 2,050m

Delhi to Manali

Half-day Delhi tour, evening Volvo coach.

Day 3 2,050m

Manali

Relax and explore.

Day 4 3,360m

Manali to Chatru

Over Rohtang into Lahaul; camp on the banks of the Chandra river.

Day 5 4,558m

Chatru to Kaza via Kunzum La

Cross Kunzum La into Spiti.

Day 6 3,650m

Kaza

Ki monastery, Kibber village and the Chao Chao Kang Nilda viewpoint.

Day 7 3,650m

Kaza — Dhankar, Tabo, Ki

Tabo monastery, "the Ajanta of the Himalayas", over a thousand years old.

Day 8 4,300m

Kaza to Chandratal

Camp by the crescent lake.

Day 9 3,080m

Chandratal to Keylong

Through Lahaul villages; hike to Khardong monastery.

Day 10 2,650m

Keylong to Udaipur

Explore Udaipur village and the Mrikula Devi temple.

Day 11 3,980m

Udaipur to Manali via Rohtang Pass

Return to Manali.

Day 12 2,050m

Manali to Delhi

Evening Volvo coach.

Day 13 216m

Delhi departure

Transfer to airport.
