Spiti Valley Cycling Tour

⏱ 15 days ▲ 4,558m Challenging Summer: June to October Minimum 6 people
Overview

An 800km cycling expedition from Manali to Shimla through Lahaul and Spiti, the largest district in Himachal Pradesh, bordered by Ladakh, Tibet, Kinnaur and the Kullu valley. One of the toughest routes even for experienced riders, it passes through the former kingdom of Spiti, where colourful Buddhist gompas dominate village life.
