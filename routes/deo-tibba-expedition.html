Deo Tibba Expedition

⏱ 14 days ▲ 6,001m Technical 15 April to 30 October Minimum 10 persons per group Heritage route
Overview

Deo Tibba is a 6,001m peak in the Pir Panjal range near Manali, capped by an extensive icefield. The climb itself is a snow hump, reached once the edge of the ice plateau is gained — a modest technical challenge that rewards climbers with a summit just over the coveted 6,000m mark.

Day by day

Day 1 2,050m

Delhi to Manali

Arrive in Manali, check in and rest. Gear preparation.

Day 2 2,050m

Manali — acclimatisation

Visit the Hadimba temple and the Vashisht hot springs while double-checking expedition gear.

Day 3 3,350m

Manali to Chikka

Drive 12km to the tunnel point, then trek 3km to the green camp at Chikka.

Day 4 3,800m

Chikka to Seri

An 8km gradual climb up the valley floor to the wide, flat meadow at Seri.

Day 5 4,480m

Seri to Chandertaal — Deo Tibba base

Trek over moraine to the small high-altitude lake of Chandertaal at the foot of Deo Tibba — the source of the Duhangan river.

Day 6 4,480m

Deo Tibba base — glacier training

Move onto the Deo Tibba glacier to practise ascending and descending technique on snow and ice.

Day 7 4,480m

Deo Tibba base — training and prep

A second day of technique on the glacier, followed by evening preparation and briefing for the climb.

Day 8 5,200m

Base camp to Camp 1

A gradual ascent to Camp 1, finishing with roughly 120m of fixed-rope climbing. Early dinner, overnight in tents.

Day 9 6,001m

Summit day — Deo Tibba

A 2am start: gradual climbing, then a 150m gully on fixed rope, more gradual ground, and a final 50m fixed-rope section to the summit. Descend safely to Camp 1.

Day 10 4,480m

Camp 1 to base camp

Descend to base camp; the rest of the day is free.

Day 11 4,480m

Weather buffer day

A spare day held in reserve for bad weather.

Day 12 3,350m

Base camp to Chikka

Descend the valley back to Chikka.

Day 13 2,050m

Chikka to Manali

An easy trek to the tunnel point, then the drive down to Manali.

Day 14 216m

Manali to Delhi

Time in Manali town, then the evening departure for Delhi.
