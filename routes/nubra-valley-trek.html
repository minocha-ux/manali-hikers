Nubra Valley Trek

⏱ 8 days ▲ 5,400m Challenging June to October Lasermo La (5,400m)
Overview

The Nubra valley, north of Leh, has a landscape found nowhere else — a desert floor of sand dunes roamed by double-humped Bactrian camels, ringed by peaks of over 6,000m in the Karakoram range. The trek enters via the Lasermo La (5,400m), with fine views over the Indus and Shyok valleys, on ground that was once an important Silk Route stopover.

Day by day

Day 1 3,500m

Arrive in Leh

Arrive and rest to acclimatise.

Day 2 3,700m

Leh to Phyang to Phyang Phu

Drive to the ancient Phyang monastery, then a short walk to Phyang Phu.

Day 3 4,700m

Phyang Phu to South base of Lasermo La

A gradual climb through high-altitude wildlife country.

Day 4 5,400m

South base to North base via Lasermo La

An early start over the pass, then a descent through glacier and moraine to a meadow camp.

Day 5 4,000m

North base to Hunder Dok

An easy meadow walk along the Hunder stream.

Day 6 3,800m

Hunder Dok to Skarchen

Past the Nubra villages of Dok Gongma, Dok Yongma and Wachan.

Day 7 3,100m

Skarchen to Hunder

Through a gorge that opens dramatically into the Shyok valley.

Day 8 3,500m

Hunder to Leh

Drive back to Leh.
