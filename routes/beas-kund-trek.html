Beas Kund Trek

⏱ 6 days ▲ 3,650m Easy Monsoon: July to September Minimum 2, maximum 14 persons
Overview

Starting from Manali, this trek winds through the famous Solang Valley, a hub for winter sports 15 kilometres from town, before continuing via Dhundi and Bakarthach. Along the way are lush meadows, glaciers and snow-capped peaks, ending at the Beas Kund glacier — the birthplace of the River Beas.

Day by day

Day 1 2,050m

Manali

Arrival, then Hidimba temple, old Manali and the Vashisht hot-spring village.

Day 2 3,300m

Manali to Solang to Dhundi to Bakarthach

Drive to Solang Nallah and Dhundi, then walk to Bakarthach — base of the surrounding peaks (Shetidhar, Ladakhi, Friendship, Hanuman Tibba).

Day 3 3,650m

Bakarthach to Beas Kund

A gradual ascent over moraine to the small glacial lake at Beas Kund, the birthplace of the Beas river.

Day 4 3,650m

Beas Kund

An easy exploration walk around the lake and back to camp.

Day 5 2,050m

Beas Kund to Manali

Trek down to Dhundi and drive to Manali.

Day 6 2,050m

Manali — departure

Transfer for onward journey.
