Leh–Ladakh Mountain Biking

⏱ 17 days ▲ 5,328m Challenging July to mid-August Minimum 6 people
Overview

A 490km expedition from Manali to Leh — one of the ultimate rides for an experienced mountain biker. Crossing the Himalayas south to north, the route traverses four of the world's highest passes, including Tanglang La at 5,328m, the world's second-highest motorable pass, through the isolated Karakoram and Zanskar ranges above the Indus valley.
