Kinner Kailash Trek

⏱ 7 days ▲ 5,300m Challenging June to October Minimum 2, maximum 14 persons
Overview

This trek in Kinnaur completes a circuit around the revered Kinnaur Kailash mountain, on trails only opened to trekkers in recent years and consequently far less used. The Kinnaur Kailash range is dominated by the Kinnaur Kailash (6,349m) and Jorkarden (6,473m) peaks, and the route's only pass, the Charang La, sits at 5,300m.

Day by day

Day 1 2,200m

Arrival in Shimla

Arrive and rest.

Day 2 2,760m

Shimla to Sangla to Thangi

A long drive via the district town of Recong Peo.

Day 3 2,890m

Thangi to Lambar

Alpine scenery, part of the trail passing through a grand gorge.

Day 4 3,500m

Lambar to Charang

A continued ascent to the large village named after the nearby pass.

Day 5 4,420m

Charang to Lalanti

To the shepherds' meadow campsite at the base of Charang La.

Day 6 5,300m

Lalanti to Chitkul via Charang La

A demanding climb with spectacular views from the pass, then a steep descent to the warm camp at Chitkul.

Day 7 2,200m

Chitkul to Shimla

Drive back to Shimla.
