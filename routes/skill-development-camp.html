Skill Development Camp

⏱ 5–7 days Easy March to November Minimum 20 persons
Overview

Based at Camp Manali, this outdoor experiential programme helps participants discover life skills, leadership and team spirit away from the comforts of home. Combining rock climbing, river crossing, snow hiking, paragliding and rafting on the Beas, it builds resilience and coping ability that carries over into everyday life.
