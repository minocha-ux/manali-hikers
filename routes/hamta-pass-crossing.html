Hamta Pass Crossing

⏱ 5 days ▲ 4,270m Moderate Monsoon: July to September Minimum 2, maximum 14 persons Heritage route
Overview

Starting from Prini village near Manali, this trek crosses the Hamta Pass to the headwaters of the Chandra river in Lahaul, skirting the lower reaches of the Bara Shigri glacier. The approach to the pass offers fine views of Deo Tibba and Indrasan along the way.

Day by day

Day 1 1,960m

Delhi to Manali

Overnight train then drive from Anandpur Sahib to Manali.

Day 2 3,100m

Manali to Chika

Jeep to Jobra, then a gradual 2-hour walk uphill and downhill, crossing streams to the Hampta river.

Day 3 3,700m

Chika to Balu Ghera

3–4 hours of short ups and downs.

Day 4 4,270m

Balu Ghera to Hamta Pass to Shiyagaru

Cross Hamta Pass and descend into the Chandra valley, with views of Deo Tibba, Indrasan and the Lahaul valleys.

Day 5 1,960m

Shiyagaru to Chatru to Manali

River crossings and a 3–4 hour descent to Chatru, then drive back to Manali over Rohtang.
