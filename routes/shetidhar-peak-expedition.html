Shetidhar Peak Expedition

⏱ 9 days ▲ 5,200m Technical 15 April to 30 October Minimum 5 persons per group
Overview

Starting from Manali, this climb winds through the Solang Valley, 15km from town, then on via Dhundi and Bakarthach to the Beas Kund glacier, birthplace of the River Beas. From the summit of Shetidhar Peak, climbers are rewarded with a panoramic view across the Dhauladhar and Pir Panjal ranges.

Day by day

Day 1 2,050m

Delhi to Manali

Arrive and visit Hidimba temple.

Day 2 2,050m

Manali

Acclimatisation walk and expedition prep.

Day 3 3,300m

Manali to Solang to Dhundi to Bakarthach

Base of the surrounding peaks.

Day 4 3,650m

Bakarthach to Beas Kund

Over moraine to the source lake of the Beas.

Day 5 3,650m

Beas Kund — glacier training

Snow ascending and descending practice.

Day 6 4,500m

Beas Kund to Camp 1

A steep ascent to establish Camp 1.

Day 7 5,200m

Camp 1 to Shetidhar Peak and back to Beas Kund

A 2am start, roped climb to the summit with panoramic Indrasan and Deo Tibba views.

Day 8 2,050m

Beas Kund to Manali

Descend to Dhundi and drive to Manali.

Day 9 216m

Manali to Delhi

Evening bus to Delhi.
