Friendship Peak

⏱ 7 days ▲ 5,289m Technical March to November Minimum 2, maximum 14 persons
Overview

The Beas Kund glacier, approached from Manali, is one of the region's favourite areas for mountaineering and ski touring. Friendship Peak, within it, is an ideal non-technical climb for those with limited or no mountaineering experience, reached from the road head at Solang Nullah.

Day by day

Day 1 2,050m

Arrival in Manali

Arrive and rest.

Day 2 3,300m

Manali to Solang to Dhundi to Bakarthach

Drive to Solang Nallah, hub of local adventure activities, then jeep to Dhundi and on to Bakarthach.

Day 3 3,600m

Glacier training at Beas Kund

Practice ascending and descending on snow and ice, dry-fruit distribution and prep for the days ahead.

Day 4 4,500m

Steep ascent to Camp 1

Pack-lunch day establishing Camp 1, with an acclimatisation walk after evening tea.

Day 5 4,500m

Rest and acclimatisation day

Rest at Camp 1.

Day 6 5,289m

Summit day — Friendship Peak

Start around 2am, roped climbing to the summit (~6 hours) — panoramic views of the Dhauladhar and Pir Panjal ranges, Indrasan and Deo Tibba — then back to Camp 1.

Day 7 2,050m

Camp 1 to Dhundi to Manali

Descend to Dhundi and drive back to Manali.
