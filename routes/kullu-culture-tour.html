Kullu Culture Tour

⏱ By enquiry
Overview

Known as 'the land of gods', the Kullu valley's fairs, customs and beliefs reflect a way of life lived close to nature. Villages worship local deities — sages, snake gods and figures from ancient scripture — in temples renowned for their carving, and the valley's folk arts, dances and songs carry a rich tradition of craftsmanship built up over centuries.
