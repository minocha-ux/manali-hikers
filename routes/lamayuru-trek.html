Lamayuru Trek

⏱ 25 days ▲ 5,120m Challenging May to October Singe La (5,120m)
Overview

The longest trek in the programme, running 25 days from Delhi and Manali through the Zanskar valley to Lamayuru. The route crosses Shingo La and Singe La, passes the cliff monastery of Phuktal and the villages of Padum, Lingshed and Photoksar, and finishes with a monastery tour around Leh.
