Manali–Leh Jeep Safari

⏱ 8 days ▲ 5,359m Easy
Overview

A classic overland route across lofty Himalayan ranges, village life and dramatic scenery, running from Manali to Leh via Rohtang Pass, Sarchu and the high Tibetan plateau at Tanglang La. The best season for the crossing runs from June to mid-October, with camping along the way and monastery visits around Leh.

Day by day

Day 1 216m

Delhi arrival

Transfer to hotel, overnight stay.

Day 2 2,050m

Delhi to Manali

Evening drive by AC coach, overnight journey.

Day 3 2,050m

Manali

Acclimatise, visit Hadimba Devi Temple, explore the mall road.

Day 4 4,290m

Manali to Sarchu

Via Rohtang Pass and Keylong; deluxe tent camp at Sarchu.

Day 5 3,500m

Sarchu to Leh

Over Tanglang La (5,359m) via the Moore Plains, the high Tibetan Plateau.

Day 6 3,500m

Leh — Shey, Thiksey, Hemis

Sightseeing at three of Ladakh's most important monasteries.

Day 7 5,600m

Leh — Khardung La

The highest motorable road in the world; views over the Indus valley and the Zanskar range.

Day 8 216m

Leh to Delhi

Flight to Delhi, international transfer.
