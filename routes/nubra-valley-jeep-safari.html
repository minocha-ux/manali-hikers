Nubra Valley Jeep Safari

⏱ 11 days ▲ 5,600m Easy
Overview

Nubra Valley, north of Leh, is reached via the highest motorable road in the world, Khardung La (5,600m), with views over the Saser Spur, the Zanskar range and the Himalayan chain. The valley itself sits at a milder 3,000m and was once the last staging post for caravan trade with Central Asia.
