Spiti Valley Motorbike Tour

⏱ 10 days ▲ 4,558m Moderate Summer: June to October Minimum 6 people
Overview

A motorbike tour into the Spiti Valley riding through the Banjar Valley and into Kinnaur, with highlights including the striking Bhimakali Temple at Sarahan and the village of Nako, close to the Tibetan border, before crossing into Spiti itself via Kaza and Chandratal Lake.

Day by day

Day 1 2,000m

Manali

Airport welcome, meet the team, local sightseeing.

Day 2 2,000m

Manali

Royal Enfield briefing; Solang, Naggar Castle, Roerich museum and Vashisht springs.

Day 3 2,683m

Manali to Banjar Valley (Soja)

Via Bhuntar and the Manikaran hot springs to the Banjar valley.

Day 4 3,134m

Banjar to Sarahan via Jalori Pass

Over Jalori Pass; the 800-year-old Bhimakali temple.

Day 5 3,300m

Sarahan to Kalpa

Temples and views over the Kinner Kailash range.

Day 6 3,663m

Kalpa to Nako

The lakeside village of Nako in Kinnaur.

Day 7 3,700m

Nako to Kaza

The ancient Dhankar monastery en route; Key monastery and Kibber nearby.

Day 8 4,300m

Kaza to Chatru

A day ride to Chandratal Lake, then on to camp at Chatru.

Day 9 3,080m

Chatru to Keylong

A hotel overlooking the valley.

Day 10 3,980m

Keylong to Manali

Over Rohtang; farewell dinner.

Priced by conversation, not by catalogue.
