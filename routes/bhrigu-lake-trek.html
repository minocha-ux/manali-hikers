Bhrigu Lake Trek

⏱ 3 days ▲ 4,240m Easy Monsoon: July to September Minimum 2, maximum 14 persons
Overview

Bhrigu Lake is a prominent glacial lake of the Kullu region, just east of Rohtang Pass. Legend holds that the sage Bhrigu meditated here, and local deities — most notably Guru Vashisht — are said to visit its sacred waters every two or three years. The trek starts at Gulaba, through forests of fir and oak, with steep ups and downs rewarded by breathtaking views.

Day by day

Day 1 2,900m

Manali to Gulaba to Kothi Theli

Drive up the Rohtang highway to Gulaba, then climb through dense deodar forest to camp.

Day 2 4,240m

Kothi Theli to Bhrigu Lake and back

Ascend slowly to the sacred glacial lake, spend time there, then return to camp.

Day 3 2,050m

Kothi Theli to Manali

Walk back to Gulaba and drive down to Manali.
