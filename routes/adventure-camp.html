Adventure Camp

⏱ 7 days ▲ 3,800m Easy March to November Minimum 20 persons
Overview

An adventure camp in the western Himalayas designed to build discipline, coordination and teamwork alongside physical development, combined with exploration of the valley's culture, earthquake-resistant architecture and ancient temples. The region is predominantly farming country, known for its apple orchards, with a Hindu majority living alongside a Buddhist minority migrated from Tibet and neighbouring districts.

Day by day

Day 1 2,000m

Delhi to Manali to Jagatsukh

Drive to the heritage village of Jagatsukh; Gaytri Devi and old Shiva temples.

Day 2 2,000m

Rock climbing and rappelling

A day on the crags, with an optional afternoon trek.

Day 3 3,350m

AD tunnel point to Chikka

Trek in with an evening river-crossing session; overnight tents.

Day 4 3,800m

Chikka to Seri

A gradual ascent to the scenic Seri campsite.

Day 5 4,300m

Seri to Chandertaal and back

To the source lake below Deo Tibba, then back to camp.

Day 6 3,350m

Seri to Chikka

An easy return with beautiful views.

Day 7 2,000m

Chikka to Manali

Through pine forest with bird's-eye views of the Beas valley.
