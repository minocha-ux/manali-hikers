# Manali Hikers — Positioning Brief

**Target:** International affluent trekkers (US / Europe) booking a once-in-a-lifetime
private Himalayan trip. English-first. Decide on authenticity, photography, and safety.

**USP:** A 4-generation Khampa nomad family that helped *map* the Himalayan passes
others now sell. "Trail knowledge passed down, not looked up." Uncopyable provenance.

**Model:** Premium, low-volume, private / small-group. **By enquiry — no prices, no cart,
no fixed departures.** Scarcity and privacy ARE the luxury signal.
